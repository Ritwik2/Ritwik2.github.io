My fascination with **data and statistics** began early, driven by a desire to understand the hidden patterns and narratives within complex numbers. This deep-seated curiosity laid the groundwork for my career, viewing data not just as information, but as the raw potential for **intelligent, scalable systems**.

This enthusiasm evolved into a professional specialization in **Data Engineering** on the **Google Cloud Platform (GCP)**.
